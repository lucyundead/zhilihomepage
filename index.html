Zhi Li (李智)

Theoretical and Computational Astrophysicist

I am a TDLI Prize Postdoc fellow at Tsung-Dao Li Institute, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My current research interests focus on gas dynamics in our Milky Way and nearby isolated galaxies using numerical simulations. This would help us better understand many problems as the origin of various features in galaxies, the formation of supermassive black holes, and the nature of dark matter. More information could be found in my CV and research.
